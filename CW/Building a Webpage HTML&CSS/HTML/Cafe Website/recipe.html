Cafe Recipes - World's Best Scrambled Eggs

Try out my new recipe below for the best scrambled eggs in the world

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving:

  • 2 eggs
    •       
  • 1 tablespoon of butter (10g)
    •       
  • 1/3 cup of cream (2 3/4 fl ounces)
    •       
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, cream and salt in a bowl.
    2.       
  2. Melt the butter in a non-stick pan over a high heat.
    3.       
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan.
    4.       
  4. Using a wooden spatula, bring the mixture into the center.
    5.       
  5. Fold contents in again, leave for 20 seconds.
  6. Grind a light sprinklig of freshly milled pepper.
